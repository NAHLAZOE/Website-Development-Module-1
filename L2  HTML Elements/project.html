Our Lady of Mount Carmel Maryhill Girls High School

Maryhill Girls is located along the Thika-Mang'u Highway. it is one of the best performing national schools in Kenya. Maryhill was founded by the White Sisters in 1933. Great women have studied from this prestigious school. A good example is the former Cabinet Secretary of Health Dr. Susan Nakumicha.

The current principle of Maryhill is Madam Ngure. She is very nice. We have three deputy principles due to our high population of more than three thousand students. We have a total of 17 subjects learnt in the school. My favourite subjects are Biology, Chemistry and Physics. In my school, Form Ones have the largest populatin of 882. Teachers in my school teach us very well and are very hardworking too.

I LOVE MY SCHOOL; MARYHILL GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL!
